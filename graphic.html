Student Orientation




During my sophomore year, I worked for the Student Experience Office and had the opportunity to create the t-shirts and other material (posters, pamphlets, etc.) for the upcoming student orientation.

The theme was "Infinite Possibilities," and I created a space-themed design. The top shirt reads "To infinity and beyond," and the two on the bottom are for the Navigating Rensselaer & Beyond (NRB) program. The shirts will be distributed to the incoming class of 2018, consisting of approximately 1500 students.

This project was completed for the Student Experience Office @ Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Spring 2014.

Grand Marshall Week




Every year in April, RPI hosts GM Week to celebrate the Grand Marshall and to complete the elections for the upcoming school year. The celebration is entirely student run. Here are some of the material I designed for the 2014 event. The theme was Vikings. Each student receives a mug when they submit their ballot to vote.

This project was completed for the GM Week Committee @ Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Spring 2014.
