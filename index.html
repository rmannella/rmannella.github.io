Projects

Graphics

A compilation of different graphic design projects ranging from poster design to t-shirt design for different clubs, friends, and employers.

Kübu

Interactive modular furniture designed specifically for children. We were inspired by memories of building forts as a kid and concern that so many children today are absorbed in tehcnology, stuck in front of screens. We want to get kids up and using their imaginations.

Pod

A green lighting project designed for Nanyang Technological University's School of Art, Design, & Media in Singapore. This installation provides an outdoor space for students to sit and lounge with friends.

Hex

After taking apart a dollar store clock, the shell was redesigned and electronics reinstalled. The focus was designing for manufacturing.
