Work-In Process...

(Don't mind the mess)


Engineer. Designer. World Traveller. Wannabe New Yorker.

I am currently a senior at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute studying Mechanical Engineering and Design, Innovation, and Society. This dual major places me at the crossroads between technicality and design.

I speak engineer. I speak design.

I spent my junior year studying abroad, first in Denmark then in Singapore, and it was one of the best experiences of my life. I visited an incredible number of places and observed people all of the world. I am passionate about exploring and live by the motto "get up and go."

I am independent, hard working, and I ask a lot of questions. I pick things up very quickly and given any task, I get it done.



Projects

Graphics

A compilation of different graphic design projects ranging from poster design to t-shirt design for different clubs, friends, and employers.

Kübu

Interactive modular furniture designed specifically for children. We were inspired by memories of building forts as a kid and concern that so many children today are absorbed in tehcnology, stuck in front of screens. We want to get kids up and using their imaginations.

Pod

A green lighting project designed for Nanyang Technological University's School of Art, Design, & Media in Singapore. This installation provides an outdoor space for students to sit and lounge with friends.

Hex

After taking apart a dollar store clock, the shell was redesigned and electronics reinstalled. The focus was designing for manufacturing.

ToolBook

The redesign of the pencil case, ToolBook is a similar shape to a textbook allowing for easy, non-bulky storage in a backpack.
