ToolBook is a redesign of the pencil case. Unlike common cases that take up an awkward amount of space in a backpack and that shake around when a backpack moves, ToolBook sits upright in the user's bag and takes up as much space as a textbook.

The return slot allows pens to be put back without the removal of the case from the bag allowing user's to leave a room in a hurry while still keeping supplies organized.

This project was completed as part of PDI Studio Three in Fall 2013.
